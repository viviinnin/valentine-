A Love Letter To The Most Beautiful Woman

I used to think that not knowing how to begin when I wanted to write to you was a bad thing. My fingers would hover over my keyboard, searching for the first words, the first line of the poem I wanted to give you, thinking, “How do I start?”

I could list a thousand things, your warmth, your laughter, the vast beauty of your mind and heart, but even that would only scratch the surface.

I could try to label these things as “reasons why I love you,” but even that would feel incomplete. Something would always be missing.

Because the way I feel about you lives in a language only my soul knows. There isn’t a phrase, a sentence, or even a dictionary entry that could explain it fully. Why I love you is a feeling too deep for words. And yet, I love you, not just for reasons I can name, but simply because I just do.

I could spend every day trying to put it all into words, and still fall short. But even in silence, even when I cannot articulate the depth of it, know that my love for you is infinite and wholly yours.

Forever yours, Vivi.
